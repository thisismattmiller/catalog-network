About
This is an early prototype for an interface into exploring the NYPL catalog. By compiling shared MARC Subject Headings into nodes of a network and simulating how they self organize this tool provides a novel look at NYPL resources. Click the search field above to look for a specific subject and click on the nodes (circles) to link back to the catalog. The entire tool is in active development, this is a first look at new possible interfaces to the library. Built by Matt Miller at NYPL Labs. March 2014
